Sapphire

Brooklyn was never the type of girl to obsess over romance. In fact, ever since she was little, it's something she's only thought about. So, she was caught more than a little off-guard when her high school's most popular boy asked her out. As they move away for college to start a new life, it's about time to figure out this relationship. With help from friends she makes along the way, Brooklyn eventually finds out more about the hidden girl deep inside her she didn't know existed and what she actually wants out of a romance, much to her surprise.

Sapphire is an LGTBQ+ "romance" novel that... really isn't. It's designed to be centered around a romance and exploration thereof, but it's so much more an exploration of personal discovery and growth, or friendships, of acceptance. All in all, Sapphire is, to me, a means of communicating parts of my story and others' and providing a lens into those while also allowing me to have the catharsis of exporting my own struggles to her in hopes of having a different world to process them in.

This project is currently in progress.
